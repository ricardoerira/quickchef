Privacy Policy for QuickChef

Introduction

At QuickChef, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy outlines the types of information we collect and how it is used in our app, which uses Firebase to enhance user experience and functionality.

Information We Collect

We collect personal information such as:

  • Account information (email, username, etc.)
  • Usage data (preferences, interactions with recipes, etc.)
  • Firebase Authentication and real-time database data for storing and synchronizing your preferences and recipes

How We Use Your Information

Your information is used to:

  • Personalize your experience on QuickChef
  • Store your preferences and recipe history in Firebase
  • Improve app functionality and provide better services

Firebase Services

QuickChef uses Firebase, a platform by Google, to store user data, authenticate users, and manage real-time interactions. Firebase’s privacy policy can be reviewed here.

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as necessary to provide our services, improve the app, or comply with legal obligations. If you wish to delete your data, you can request it through the app's settings or contact support.

Cookies

QuickChef may use cookies to improve your experience by remembering your preferences. You can manage or disable cookies through your browser settings.

Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously. We use Firebase’s built-in security features to protect your data from unauthorized access.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update our Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will post the updated policy here. Please review this page periodically for any changes.
